How to use the Energy Consumption Calculator?

  1. Check your latest TNB bill for the Import and Export values from your current month and next month's bill.
      Refer to the first image below to locate where to find these values on your bill.

  2. From your solar inverter monitoring apps, look for your Solar Generation value.
      3. The second image shows where you can typically find this value.

  3. Once you have these three values (Import, Export, Solar Generation), input them into the calculator. It will show the possible reason of why the TNB bill is higher.
